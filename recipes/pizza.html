Gluten Free Pizza Recipe

This gluten free pizza recipe is easy to made and absolutely delicious! Beginners o gluten free recipes will find this recipe easy to make. Enjoy!

Note: if you would like an extra cripsy crust, it would be a good idea to invest in a pizza stone.

Ingredients

  • 250g Caputo Fioreglut Gluten Free Flour
  • 9g salt
  • 5g instant yeast
  • 200g warm water
  • 7.5g olive oil
  • Your favorite pizza sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix together the warm water and the yeast in a small mixing bowl and let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, combine the flour and salt in a separate large mixing bowl.
  3. After 5 minutes, stir the oil into the wet ingredients bowl.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the flour mixture. Stir (either by hand or in a stand mixer) for 3-4 minutes or until thoroughly combined.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let rise for 2 hours.
  6. After 2 hours, preheat the oven to 500 F.
  7. Flour your work surface, rub oil on your hands and then knead the dough for 2 minutes.
  8. Split the dough and create two even balls.
  9. On a generously floured pizza peel, place one of the dough balls in the center.
  10. Using your hands and working from the middle, press the dough outwards until you have a thin (about 1/4 inch thick) round crust.
  11. Add toppings to the pizza and then slide onto a pizza stone in the preheated oven.
  12. Cook for approximately 13 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
  13. Remove and enjoy!
